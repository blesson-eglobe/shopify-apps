Overview

Mealbot Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for Mealbot. This guide will help you set up and optimize your subscription-based meal delivery service using our Shopify app.

Pro Tip: Mealbot is built natively on Shopify Subscriptions. Ensure you have the Shopify Subscriptions extension enabled in your store settings before proceeding.

About the App

Mealbot is designed to simplify the complexities of managing recurring meal plans. From dietary preferences to delivery scheduling, it automates the tedious administrative work so you can focus on your kitchen operations.

Setup

Installation

Follow these simple steps to integrate Mealbot into your Shopify store in minutes.

1

Shopify App Store

Search for "Mealbot" in the Shopify App Store and click "Add App". Follow the prompts to authorize installation.

2

Theme Integration

In the Mealbot dashboard, click "Enable Theme Extension". This automatically adds the meal selection widget to your product pages.

3

Permission Sync

Review and approve the necessary permissions for managing draft orders and subscription contracts.

Config

App Configuration

Configure your subscription logic and meal mapping to match your business model.

Setting up Plans

Navigate to Settings > Subscription Plans. Here you can define:

  • Frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
  • Discount rates for long-term commitments
  • Cut-off times for meal selections
// Example configuration for a Weekly Plan
{
  "plan_name": "Weekly Healthy Glow",
  "frequency": "weekly",
  "min_meals": 5,
  "max_meals": 12,
  "discount": 10
}
Capabilities

Core Features

Powerful tools specifically curated to handle the unique challenges of meal prep services.

Dynamic Mapping

Automatically map your Shopify products to specific meal categories and delivery days.

Self-Service Portal

Allow customers to skip weeks, pause subscriptions, or swap meals independently.

Kitchen Reports

Generate precise production lists based on upcoming meal selections across all active contracts.

Process

Operational Workflow

How the app handles the end-to-end journey from selection to delivery.

A

Order Placement

Customer selects their plan and meal preferences on your storefront. Shopify native contract is created.

B

Recurrence Trigger

On the billing date, Mealbot triggers the next billing cycle and sends a meal selection reminder.

C

fulfillment Sync

Selection data is pushed to your kitchen staff via CSV export or direct integration.
