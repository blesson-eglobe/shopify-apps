Core Features

Complete Negotiation Control

Everything your business needs to manage the entire quoting lifecycle across admin and storefront.

Interactive Quote Cart

  • Frontend widget allowing product additions
  • Customers manage quote items and modify quantities
  • Input form for expected final pricing

Customer Price Requests

  • Directly submit quote applications to admin
  • Track quote status (pending, approved, rejected)
  • Revise quotes with configurable attempts allowed

Centralized Admin Hub

  • Centralized Quote Dashboard for incoming requests
  • View full quote details including products and notes
  • Analyze historical quotation success rates

Approval Workflow

  • Approve or reject quotes based on defined rules
  • Adjust pricing counter-offers before final authorization
  • Lock-in validity window for approved discounts

Professional Deliverables

  • Generate comprehensive PDFs of finalized quotes
  • Allow customer to download quotes natively
  • Customize email templates used in correspondence

Secure Shopify Checkout

  • Convert quotes directly into Shopify Draft Orders
  • Send fully secure and automated checkout links
  • Bypass general store pricing seamlessly
Flow

Step-by-Step Purchasing Journey

See exactly how QuoteBuilder connects the dots from initial interest directly to paid order.

1

🛒 Add & Request

The customer adds products of interest to their quote list and submits their expected custom price.

2

📩 Notification & Review

The admin receives an instant notification detailing the quote, the desired discount, and any attached notes.

3

🔍 Negotiate & Action

The admin reviews the numbers and can either approve, reject, or present an alternative counter-offer pricing scheme.

4

✅ Approval & Authorization

Upon final agreement and approval, an automatic checkout link is generated utilizing Shopify native draft orders.

5

💳 Secure Payment

The customer uses the secure link to seamlessly complete payment, and the final order is fully created in Shopify.
