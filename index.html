QuoteBuilder

The QuoteBuilder app in Shopify allows customers to add products to a quote and request a custom discount on the total order value. The requested discount is applied to the overall product total, which the merchant can review and approve based on business rules. This enables flexible pricing, supporting negotiation-driven sales while maintaining control over final pricing decisions.
